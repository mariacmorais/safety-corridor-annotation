Ground Truth Safety Corridor Annotation

Review the assigned operative clip, then draw two lines: one at the most critical superior point for incision, and one at the most critical inferior point for incision, as to create a "safety corridor" indicating a safety zone for initiating the incision. When you submit, your annotation is sent directly to the study team.

1. Enter Your Last Name and Institution

Enter your last name. This is required before submitting your annotation.

2. Select Clip

Please review and annotate every clip.

3. Watch Clip

Select a clip to begin playback.

4. Annotate Final Frame

Tap or click to place the lines, drag, and release to finish. You can begin as soon as the frozen frame appears, even if the clip is still playing above.

The final frame appears below shortly. You can keep watching while it prepares.

5. Enter the Parkland Score

Based on the clip, enter your impression of the severity of the case according to the Parkland Grading Scale (1-4).

6. Enter the Nassar Score

Based on the clip, enter your impression of the difficulty of the case according to the Nassar Grading Scale (1-4).

7. Submit Annotations

Draw the lines on the frozen frame to enable submission.
